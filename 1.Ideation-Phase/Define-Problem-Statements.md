## Customer Problem Statement

Organizations often face delays and inefficiencies in employee onboarding and offboarding because the process depends on manual coordination between HR, managers, IT, Facilities, and Security teams. Approvals, task assignments, and status tracking are usually handled through emails, spreadsheets, or phone calls, making the process slow, error-prone, and difficult to monitor. This can result in delayed account creation, incomplete asset allocation, security risks, and poor employee experience.

To address these challenges, an automated Employee Lifecycle Management solution is required. The system should streamline request submission, automate manager approvals, assign tasks to the appropriate departments, track SLA compliance, provide real-time visibility through dashboards and reports, and ensure secure, role-based access. By digitizing the entire lifecycle, organizations can improve operational efficiency, transparency, compliance, and employee satisfaction.
