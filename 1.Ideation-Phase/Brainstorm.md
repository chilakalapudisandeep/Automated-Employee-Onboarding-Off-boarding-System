# Step 1: Team Gathering, Collaboration & Select the Problem Statement

### Team Discussion Summary

The team discussed the challenges faced during employee onboarding and offboarding in organizations. The existing process involves multiple departments such as HR, IT, Facilities, and Security, leading to delays, manual approvals, poor communication, and lack of centralized tracking. After evaluating different ideas, the team selected **Employee Lifecycle Management using ServiceNow** as the best solution because it automates approvals, task assignments, SLA tracking, notifications, and reporting.
